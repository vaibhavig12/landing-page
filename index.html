Who Are We?

Situated in Goregaon East, Mumbai, Maharashtra, Pandey Sand Suppliers & Transport specializes in the supply and trade of M sand and River sand. Our sand varieties are renowned for their strength, strong adhesion, and superior quality, making them ideal for construction purposes. With a wide range available, we ensure our customers receive top-notch products at competitive prices.Backed by cutting-edge infrastructure equipped with state-of-the-art testing facilities and advanced technology, we guarantee the quality of our sands. Our team consists of highly qualified experts dedicated to meeting our clients' needs efficiently and effectively.
